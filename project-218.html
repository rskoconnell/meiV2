VA Monterey Clininc Reuse Study

Project Details
  • Client: Department of Veterans Affairs
  • Location: Monterey, CA
  • Size: 35,000 square feet
  • Status: Planning Study

The existing 35,000 SF Monterey Clinic presently serves the Veteran population of Monterey, Santa Cruz and San Benito Counties. Pending completion of the new Gourley Monterey Clinic in spring/summer 2016, current services at the existing Monterey Clinic will be relocated into its new location.

MEI was commissioned to perform a Reuse Study to identify the highest and best programmatic services for the existing Monterey Clinic in conjunction with services offered at the new Gourley Monterey Clinic. To meet current and future Veteran patient needs, the Preferred Conceptual Program and Plan facilitates a new Dental Clinic, Recreational Therapy Program with new Aquatic Therapy Pool, an Administrative Wing and Multi-purpose spaces for community use.
