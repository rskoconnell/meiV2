San Francisco International Airport Terminal 3 Secure Corridor

Project Details
  • Client: San Francisco International Airport
  • Location: San Mateo, CA

As part of San Francisco International Airport’s ongoing improvement program, a new secured corridor between Terminal 3 and the International Terminal was constructed to allow airside passengers to move between terminals. As consultants to Fong & Chan Architects, MEI provided bridging documents to the airport.
