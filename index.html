Welcome to MEI Architects

Established in 2003, MEI has grown by successfully partnering with a range of government, nonprofit, and private clients to deliver elegant, pragmatic, community-minded solutions to technically complex challenges.

The firm, built from a core strength of healthcare design, has developed a diverse portfolio of experience and built a broad team of professionals necessary to design and deliver projects across the healthcare, life science, justice, multi-family residential, commercial, retail, and aviation sectors.
