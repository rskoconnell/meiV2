Michael Wang named as Principal

By Ruairi O'Connell | May 8th 2017

MEI Architects is excited to announce that architect Michael Wang has been named a Principal. With almost forty years of experience designing and delivering civic, ‘gated residential’ (justice), aviation and healthcare projects, Michael is a luminary of both the firm and the design profession, as well as a mentor to the next generation of architects and designers.

Michael joined MEI in 2014, and most recently completed the technically challenging Chinese Hospital replacement tower in San Francisco.
