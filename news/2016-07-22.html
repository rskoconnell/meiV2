Moving Day

By Ruairi O'Connell | July 22nd 2016

As architects and designers, we recognize that space is important. As we branch out with new clients and widen the range of services we can offer our existing clients, we're working on ensuring that we ourselves have a space that supports the collaboration, communication and quiet focus necessary to perform at the highest levels.

As of today, MEI will be moving to a temporary space on the first floor of our building. We expect to be here for 8 weeks while our contractors, the excellent Vision Builders, remodel the second floor. Our address will not change, and we expect to back to business as usual on Monday. If we miss a phone call from you this afternoon, or your next meeting at MEI is in a makeshift conference room, please accept out apologies.

We look forward to welcoming our clients, our collaborators, and our staff to our new work space in the fall.
