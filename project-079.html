VA Martinez New Building Entries

Project Details
  • Client: Department of Veterans Affairs
  • Location: Martinez, CA

VA Martinez needed to integrate programs in two adjacent buildings. The proposed design solution provided secure entry vestibules for Building 20 and Building 22 and connected them with a covered walkway closed on the south side with a large window wall. The solution provides a physical connection with protection from inclement weather and increases energy performance with new entry vestibules.
